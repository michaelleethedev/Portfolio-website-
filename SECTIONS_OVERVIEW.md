## 🎨 Overall Design System

### Colors
- **Primary**: Blue (#2563eb) - buttons, accents, links
- **Secondary**: Dark Navy (#0f172a) - headings, footer
- **Text**: Dark Gray (#1e293b) - body text
- **Light Text**: Medium Gray (#64748b) - descriptions
- **Background**: White / Light Gray (#f8fafc)

### Typography
- **Font**: Inter (Google Fonts)
- **Headings**: Bold, dark navy
- **Body**: Regular, dark gray
- **Hierarchy**: Clear size scale from 48px to 14px

### Spacing
- **Sections**: Large vertical spacing (6rem)
- **Cards**: Consistent padding (2rem - 3rem)
- **Elements**: Systematic spacing scale

### Effects
- **Hover**: Subtle lift and shadow on cards
- **Transitions**: Smooth 200ms animations
- **Shadows**: Layered shadows for depth
- **Borders**: Subtle borders with hover color change

### Responsive
- **Desktop**: Full multi-column layouts
- **Tablet** (< 968px): Adjusted columns
- **Mobile** (< 768px): Single column, stacked
- **Small Mobile** (< 480px): Optimized text sizes

## 🎯 User Journey

1. **Land on Hero**: Immediate understanding of who Saleem is
2. **Read About**: Learn about approach and capabilities
3. **Scan Skills**: Quick verification of technical skills
4. **Explore Projects**: Deep dive into real work (main conversion point)
5. **Review Services**: Understand what can be offered
6. **Contact**: Easy path to get in touch

**Total Scroll Time**: ~2-3 minutes for full review
**Recruiter Scan Time**: ~30 seconds to get key information

---

## 📱 Mobile Experience

All sections adapt gracefully:
- Navigation becomes hamburger menu
- Multi-column layouts stack vertically
- Cards remain full-width for readability
- Touch-friendly button sizes
- Optimized text sizes
- Maintained visual hierarchy

## 🎓 Section Priorities

For recruiters, the most important sections are:

1. **Hero** (5 seconds): Who are you?
2. **Projects** (60 seconds): What have you built?
3. **Skills** (15 seconds): What technologies?
4. **Contact** (10 seconds): How to reach you?

The portfolio is optimized for this scanning pattern.
